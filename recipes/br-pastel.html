Brazilian Pastel Recipe


Description

Pastel is a popular Brazilian street food. A thin, crispy pastry filled with savory or sweet ingredients, then deep-fried until golden. Common fillings include cheese, meat, or hearts of palm, making it a versatile and delicious snack.

Reference to the recipe: Pastel Recipe

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour (plus more for dusting - see tips below)
  • Warm Water
  • Oil: any neutral flavored vegetable oil works.
  • White vinegar (I used apple cider vinegar)
  • Salt: add as much as you like. my version isn't very salty so if you want you can add an extra pinch.
  • Cheese: Traditionally Mozzarella cheese is used. You can add as little or as much as it will fit (without tearing) into your pastel.

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients together until a dough forms. Knead for a couple minutes until smooth.
  2. Roll it out using a rolling pin or a pasta machine.
  3. Fill with your favorite filling, fold and seal the edges.
  4. Deep fry until golden and bubbly.
